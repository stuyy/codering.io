Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I get started?
A: You must first login with GitHub.
Q: What are events hosted on Intellectual Developers?
A: Every period (1 week, 2 weeks, 4 weeks) we want to create an event where users may join together and collaborate on a small project hosted by our Discord Server staff.
Q: What kind of benefits do I get for contributing?
A: Everyone that participates will be merited points based on several factors. Each event will have one winner whom will be rewarded a prize. Prizes can range from a Raspberry Pi, Mechanical Keyboard, Amazon Gift Cards, and more!

Contribution & Points

Q: How do I receive points?
A: You will receive points each time you make a contribution on one of our GitHub projects.
Q: What is considered a "contribution"?
A: Contributions may be any of the following:

  • Providing feedback on someone's Pull Request (1 Point)
  • Opening issues regarding bug fixes (1 Point)
  • Having your Pull Request approved (2 Points)
